What is ProtoVid?

ProtoVid is a Figma plugin that exports your prototype flows as high-quality MP4 and GIF videos. It automatically detects prototype interactions in your file, records the full flow, and outputs a video you can share with stakeholders, include in documentation, or drop into a Slack channel — no live demo required.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ProtoVid? +
ProtoVid is a Figma plugin that exports your prototype flows as high-quality MP4 and GIF videos. It automatically detects prototype interactions and records the full flow.
How do I export a Figma prototype as a video? +
Install ProtoVid from the Figma Community, open a file with prototype interactions, run the plugin from the Plugins menu, choose your settings, and click Export Video.
What video formats does ProtoVid support? +
MP4 is available on both free and premium tiers. GIF export is a premium feature. MP4 is ideal for presentations; GIF is perfect for quick sharing in chat or docs.
What resolutions are available? +
Free users export at 720p. Premium users can choose 720p, 1080p, or 4K, with 30fps or 60fps frame rates.
Does ProtoVid work with Figma's free plan? +
Yes. ProtoVid works with any Figma plan — Free, Professional, Organization, or Enterprise. The plugin's free and premium tiers are separate from your Figma subscription.
