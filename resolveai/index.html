Frequently Asked Questions

What is ResolveAI? +
ResolveAI is an AI-powered customer support agent for Shopify and Zendesk. Unlike chatbots that just answer FAQs, ResolveAI autonomously handles real tasks — processing refunds, looking up orders, managing returns, and answering product questions from your catalog.
How is this different from a chatbot? +
Chatbots answer questions from a script. ResolveAI is an autonomous agent that completes tasks end-to-end. It can actually process a refund in your Shopify store, not just tell the customer how to request one.
How long does setup take? +
About 5 minutes. Install the app, connect to your store, configure your return/refund policies, and your AI employee starts working immediately. No training data or complex setup required.
What happens with complex issues? +
ResolveAI knows its limits. Complex edge cases that require human judgment get escalated to your team with full context — what the customer asked, what ResolveAI already tried, and what's needed to resolve the issue.
What's a "resolution"? +
A resolution is a complete customer interaction where ResolveAI performed a meaningful action — looking up an order, processing a refund, or answering a product question. You only pay for completed work.
Which platforms does ResolveAI support? +
ResolveAI currently supports Shopify and Zendesk. Install from either marketplace, and the AI agent integrates directly with your existing support workflow.
