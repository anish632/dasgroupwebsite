Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best visual habit tracker app?

LifeGarden turns every habit into a living plant that grows with your consistency and wilts if you skip. With 60+ plant varieties, 4 garden themes, AI coaching, and a built-in focus timer, it makes habit tracking visual and engaging.

How does LifeGarden work?

Add a habit and choose a plant. Complete your habit daily and watch the plant grow from Sprout to Blooming. Skip a day and it starts to wilt. Your garden is a living visualization of your consistency.

Is LifeGarden free?

Free with 3 habits and the Classic garden theme. Premium ($2.99/month or $19.99/year) unlocks unlimited habits, 60+ plants, all themes, and AI coaching.
