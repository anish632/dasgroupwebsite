Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Introduction

Das Group LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our products and services, including:

  • Apple apps (iOS and macOS applications distributed via the App Store)
  • Shopify apps (applications installed through the Shopify App Store)
  • HubSpot Breeze agent apps (tools and integrations for HubSpot's Breeze AI platform)
  • Notion integrations (applications connected through Notion's integration platform)
  • Figma plugins (extensions installed through the Figma Community)
  • Airtable extensions (apps installed within Airtable bases)
  • MCP servers (Model Context Protocol servers used by AI assistants and tools)
  • Any other applications, integrations, or services we offer

Collectively referred to as "our apps" or "our services" throughout this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account registration information (name, email address)
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Content you create or share through our apps
  • Communications with customer support

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Usage data and app analytics
  • Log data (IP address, access times, app features used)
  • Location data (if you grant permission)
  • Purchase and subscription information processed through platform stores (e.g., Apple App Store)

Apple App Data

When you use our iOS or macOS applications:

  • Purchase and subscription data is processed exclusively by Apple; we do not store or access your payment information
  • We may collect anonymous analytics through Apple's App Analytics or third-party analytics services
  • Any on-device data (e.g., local storage, Core Data) stays on your device unless you explicitly sync or share it
  • If the app uses Apple Sign In, we receive only the information you authorize (name and/or email)

Shopify App Data

When you install and use our Shopify applications:

  • We may collect store information (store name, domain, address, and contact details), merchant account information, product and inventory data, order and customer data, and Shopify access tokens as required for the app's functionality
  • We access and use store data solely to provide and improve the functionality of our Shopify apps
  • We do not sell merchant or customer data obtained through Shopify to third parties
  • Data collected through Shopify APIs is used in accordance with Shopify's API Terms of Service
  • Upon uninstalling our Shopify app, we will delete your store data within 30 days unless retention is required by law

HubSpot Breeze Agent App Data

When you install and use our HubSpot Breeze agent tools:

  • We receive OAuth access tokens and account identifiers when you authorize the integration
  • We access CRM data (deals, contacts, tasks, owners, and related objects) only as needed to perform the actions you request through Breeze agents
  • Deal data and contact information may be processed by third-party AI services (e.g., OpenAI) to generate analysis, recommendations, and content
  • We do not persistently store your CRM data; it is fetched in real time, processed, and returned
  • Tasks or records created in your HubSpot account by our tools are owned by you and governed by your HubSpot agreement
  • Data usage complies with HubSpot's Developer Terms
  • Upon uninstalling, OAuth tokens are revoked and we no longer have access to your HubSpot account

Notion Integration Data

When you connect our Notion integrations:

  • We access only the pages, databases, and content you explicitly share with the integration
  • Notion access tokens are stored securely and used solely to perform actions on your behalf
  • We do not access workspaces, pages, or databases that have not been shared with the integration
  • Data usage complies with Notion's API Terms of Use
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Notion settings

Figma Plugin Data

When you use our Figma plugins:

  • Plugins operate within Figma's sandboxed environment and can only access the current file data that Figma's API permits
  • We may collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the plugin
  • We do not access or store your Figma account credentials
  • Design data processed by the plugin remains within Figma unless the plugin's functionality explicitly involves exporting or transmitting data, which will be clearly disclosed
  • Data usage complies with Figma's Developer Terms

Airtable Extension Data

When you install our Airtable extensions:

  • Extensions access table, field, and record data within the base where the extension is installed, as permitted by Airtable's API
  • We only access data necessary to deliver the extension's functionality
  • We do not copy or store your Airtable data on external servers unless the extension's functionality explicitly requires it (e.g., syncing with an external service), which will be clearly disclosed
  • Data usage complies with Airtable's Developer Terms
  • Removing the extension immediately revokes its access to your base

MCP Server Data

When you use our Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers:

  • MCP servers process requests from AI assistants (e.g., Claude, ChatGPT, or other compatible tools) on your behalf
  • Data sent to our MCP servers (queries, context, and parameters) is processed in real time and not persistently stored unless caching is required for functionality
  • If the MCP server connects to third-party APIs or services, your data is transmitted to those services in accordance with their respective privacy policies
  • We do not log or retain the content of your AI conversations beyond what is necessary for error debugging (logs are retained for no more than 30 days)
  • MCP servers may be self-hosted by you, in which case your data never leaves your infrastructure

AI and Third-Party Processing

Some of our apps use artificial intelligence services to generate insights, content, or recommendations. When AI processing is involved:

  • Your data may be sent to third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) for processing
  • We send only the minimum data necessary to perform the requested action
  • AI-generated outputs are returned to you and not stored by us beyond the session
  • Third-party AI providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data sent through their APIs
  • We do not use your data to train AI models

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our apps and services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices, updates, and administrative messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Personalize and improve user experience
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our apps and conducting our business
  • Platform Providers: Information may be processed by the platforms our apps operate on (Apple, Shopify, HubSpot, Notion, Figma, Airtable, etc.) in accordance with their respective policies
  • AI Providers: Data may be sent to AI service providers for processing as described above
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure token storage, and limited data retention. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Authentication tokens are stored only while you actively use the integration and are deleted upon uninstallation or revocation
  • Usage analytics and error logs are retained for up to 90 days
  • Data processed in real time (e.g., AI analysis, MCP requests) is not stored beyond the session
  • Upon account deletion or app uninstallation, your data is deleted within 30 days unless retention is required by law

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your account information through our apps
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke any integration's access at any time through the respective platform's settings (Notion, HubSpot, Shopify, etc.)
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of certain communications and data collection
  • Location Services: You can disable location services through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
