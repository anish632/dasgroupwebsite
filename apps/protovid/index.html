Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export a Figma prototype as a video?

Install ProtoVid from the Figma Community plugins. Open any Figma file with prototype interactions, run Plugins → ProtoVid, choose your export settings (resolution, frame rate, format), and click Export Video. ProtoVid automatically detects your prototype flow and generates an MP4 or GIF video file.

Can I export Figma prototypes as GIF?

Yes. ProtoVid Pro supports GIF export in addition to MP4. GIFs are ideal for sharing in Slack, email, documentation, and social media where auto-playing video previews are needed.

What resolution does ProtoVid support?

ProtoVid supports 720p (free), 1080p (Pro), and 4K (Pro). Frame rates of 30fps and 60fps are available. 4K at 60fps produces presentation-quality video.

Does ProtoVid add a watermark?

Free exports include a small watermark. Pro ($12/month) removes it entirely for clean client deliverables and portfolio use.

How much does ProtoVid cost?

Free with 3 exports at 720p (MP4 with watermark). Pro is $12/month for unlimited exports, up to 4K resolution, MP4 + GIF, and watermark-free output.

Does ProtoVid detect flows automatically?

Yes. ProtoVid scans your Figma file and detects all prototype interactions and flows. It captures the entire sequence including transitions — no manual screen configuration needed.
