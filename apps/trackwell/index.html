Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best simple nutrition tracker app?

TrackWell Daily is a free nutrition tracker that focuses on three things: water, protein, and vegetables. Unlike calorie-counting apps, TrackWell uses one-tap logging with no food databases or barcode scanning. It includes streak tracking, AI coaching, and Apple Watch support.

Does TrackWell count calories?

No. TrackWell intentionally skips calorie counting. It tracks water intake, protein servings, and vegetable servings — the three habits that research shows have the biggest impact on how you feel day-to-day.

Does TrackWell have an Apple Watch app?

Yes. TrackWell Premium includes an Apple Watch app with complications for quick access. Log water, protein, and veggies directly from your wrist.

Is TrackWell free?

TrackWell is free with unlimited daily tracking, 7 days of history, and streak tracking up to 3 days. Premium ($4.99/month or $29.99/year) unlocks unlimited history, Watch app, family sharing, AI coaching, and more.
