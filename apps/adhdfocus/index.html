Built for How ADHD Brains Actually Work

ADHDFocus addresses the 3 core ADHD challenges: task paralysis (AI breakdown), time blindness (short timers + hyperfocus alerts), and consistency (streak tracking without shame).

  1. 1

    ADHD-Specific Timer Sessions (5/15/25 Minutes)

    Standard pomodoro apps assume 25-minute attention spans. ADHD brains often can't sustain that. ADHDFocus offers 5-minute Gentle sessions for high-resistance days, 15-minute Sprints for moderate focus, and 25-minute Deep sessions for when you're in the zone.

  2. 2

    AI Task Breakdown

    Feeling overwhelmed by "clean the apartment"? AI splits any task into concrete 5-minute steps. Tap one step, set the timer, everything else fades. This reduces executive function load — the core ADHD challenge that makes starting feel impossible.

  3. 3

    Hyperfocus Lock-In Alerts

    Hyperfocus is a real ADHD phenomenon — you lose hours on one thing while everything else burns. ADHDFocus automatically alerts you when your session ends, breaking the hyperfocus cycle before you forget to eat, drink water, or attend that meeting.

  4. 4

    Focus Analytics — When Does Your Brain Peak?

    Discover your brain's patterns without judgment. ADHDFocus tracks when you focus best — morning, evening, post-lunch — so you can schedule important work during your peak windows instead of fighting your brain.

  5. 5

    Streak Tracking Without Shame

    Most productivity apps make you feel terrible when you miss a day. ADHDFocus celebrates what you did, not what you didn't. Progress tracking that works with your ADHD instead of against it.

  6. 6

    Widgets & Live Activity

    Home screen, Lock Screen widgets, and Live Activity for active sessions. See your current timer without opening the app — reducing the distraction of unlocking your phone and getting pulled into other apps.
