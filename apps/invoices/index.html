All Features

Professional invoices in 30 seconds — now with time tracking and voice creation

  1. 1

    Time Tracking + Auto-Invoice

    Start a timer when you begin work, stop when you're done. At the end of the week, tap "Invoice" and your tracked hours become a professional invoice — line items, rates, and client details all pre-filled. Quick-start by tapping a recent client. Zero data entry.

  2. 2

    Voice Invoice Creation

    Tap the microphone and speak: "Invoice for Sarah Chen, 12 hours of web development at 150 an hour, plus 500 for the logo design." On-device speech recognition extracts the client, line items, and amounts. Three taps from start to finish. Works offline.

  3. 3

    GPS Job Sites

    Save job site locations and the timer starts automatically when you arrive, stops when you leave. Perfect for contractors, cleaners, and anyone who works at client locations.

  4. 4

    AI Smart Compose & Multiple Creation Flows

    Voice, AI text, quick, or detailed — four ways to create invoices. AI auto-detects client names, rates, quantities, and line items. Industry templates for consulting, construction, design, and cleaning.

  5. 5

    Client Management & Revenue Dashboard

    Save client details for repeat invoicing. Track total billed, pending, and paid per client. Weekly, monthly, and quarterly revenue views. Overdue invoice alerts.

  6. 6

    Professional PDF & Email

    Clean PDF invoices emailed directly from the app with AI-composed messages. Choose professional, friendly, or follow-up tone. Multiple templates. Custom branding with your logo.
