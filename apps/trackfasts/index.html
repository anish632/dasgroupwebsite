Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free intermittent fasting app for iPhone?

TrackFasts is a free intermittent fasting timer app for iPhone that supports 4+ fasting protocols (16:8, 18:6, 20:4, OMAD) with one-tap start, streak tracking, and Apple Watch support. It works 100% offline with no account required. Premium features like weight tracking and science-based insights are available via optional upgrade.

What fasting schedules does TrackFasts support?

TrackFasts supports 4+ intermittent fasting protocols: 16:8 (16 hours fasting, 8 hours eating), 18:6, 20:4, and OMAD (One Meal A Day). You can start any protocol with a single tap and switch between them at any time.

Does TrackFasts work with Apple Watch?

Yes. TrackFasts includes an Apple Watch companion app so you can start, stop, and monitor your fasting timer directly from your wrist. It also supports home screen and lock screen widgets on iPhone for at-a-glance progress.

TrackFasts is 100% private. All data stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. It works completely offline. The only data collected (usage data) is not linked to your identity, as disclosed on the App Store.

What happens to my body during intermittent fasting?

During intermittent fasting, your body transitions through several stages: fat burning typically begins after 12 hours, autophagy (cellular cleanup) starts around 16–18 hours, and deeper ketosis occurs at 20+ hours. TrackFasts provides science-based education on each stage so you understand what's happening during your fast.

Can I track my weight with TrackFasts?

Yes. TrackFasts Pro includes weight tracking with visual trend charts so you can see how intermittent fasting affects your weight over time. Milestone predictions show what's ahead based on your progress.
