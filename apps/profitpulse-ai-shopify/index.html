What It Does

Margin visibility that points toward action

The value proposition is not just tracking data. It is helping a Shopify merchant find the products and orders where profit is softer than expected and giving them a clearer next step.

Profit by order

Merchants can see profit signals at the order level instead of relying on top-line sales alone when they judge store performance.

Low-margin product alerts

The app is built to surface products and orders that deserve attention, which helps merchants spot hidden margin leaks earlier.

AI pricing guidance

ProfitPulse AI can frame pricing suggestions and margin insights so a merchant has something concrete to review instead of raw analytics alone.
